Let's be honest. **Decision Fatigue & Overthinking** is something we all deal with, but rarely admit to. We live in a society that demands perfection, punctuality, and constant productivity. But what if we leaned into the chaos instead?

## The Decision Fatigue & Overthinking Epidemic

It starts small. Paralyzed by what brand of toothpaste to buy? Exhausted by the 4:00 PM 'what's for dinner' crisis? Finally, a parody self-help guide that understands your struggle. You tell yourself it's just this once. But soon, it becomes a lifestyle. And you know what? That's okay. Because trying to be perfect is exhausting, and quite frankly, boring.

Imagine a world where we celebrated our flaws instead of hiding them. Where "Paralyzed by what brand of toothpaste to buy? This is your manifesto." wasn't just a funny saying, but a badge of honor.

## Why Satire is the Best Medicine

When life gets overwhelming, humor is our best defense mechanism. Laughing at our own struggles takes the power away from them. It makes the scary stuff feels managed, and the annoying stuff feel ridiculous.
