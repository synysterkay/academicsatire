Let's be honest. **Imposter Syndrome** is something we all deal with, but rarely admit to. We live in a society that demands perfection, punctuality, and constant productivity. But what if we leaned into the chaos instead?

## The Imposter Syndrome Epidemic

It starts small. Ever feel like success feels illegal—like you’re about to be caught for winning? A sharp self-help satire that dives into success guilt. You tell yourself it's just this once. But soon, it becomes a lifestyle. And you know what? That's okay. Because trying to be perfect is exhausting, and quite frankly, boring.

Imagine a world where we celebrated our flaws instead of hiding them. Where "Sometimes success isn’t scary — believing you earned it is." wasn't just a funny saying, but a badge of honor.

## Why Satire is the Best Medicine

When life gets overwhelming, humor is our best defense mechanism. Laughing at our own struggles takes the power away from them. It makes the scary stuff feels managed, and the annoying stuff feel ridiculous.

That's the core philosophy behind my latest book, **[Why Success Feels Illegal To Me](/books/why-success-feels-illegal-to-me/)**.
