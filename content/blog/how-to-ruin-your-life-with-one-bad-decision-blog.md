Let's be honest. **Bad Decisions & Regret** is something we all deal with, but rarely admit to. We live in a society that demands perfection, punctuality, and constant productivity. But what if we leaned into the chaos instead?

## The Bad Decisions & Regret Epidemic

It starts small. Ever wondered how to ruin your life with one bad decision? This brutally honest self-help satire explores the life-ruining mistakes. You tell yourself it's just this once. But soon, it becomes a lifestyle. And you know what? That's okay. Because trying to be perfect is exhausting, and quite frankly, boring.

Imagine a world where we celebrated our flaws instead of hiding them. Where "Confidence in bad decisions is easy—living with the consequences is not." wasn't just a funny saying, but a badge of honor.

## Why Satire is the Best Medicine

When life gets overwhelming, humor is our best defense mechanism. Laughing at our own struggles takes the power away from them. It makes the scary stuff feels managed, and the annoying stuff feel ridiculous.

That's the core philosophy behind my latest book, **[How To Ruin Your Life With One Bad Decision](/books/how-to-ruin-your-life-with-one-bad-decision/)**.
