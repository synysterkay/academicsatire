## Are You Ready to Laugh at Your Own Misery?

Ever feel like success feels illegal—like you’re about to be caught for winning? A sharp self-help satire that dives into success guilt.

In a world obsessed with perfection, high achievement, and maintaining a curated Instagram feed, **Why Success Feels Illegal To Me** comes as a breath of fresh air (or a sigh of relief). This isn't just a book; it's a mirror reflecting the hilarity of our modern struggles.

### Why You Need This Book

Let's face it: life is absurd. We stress about things that don't matter, we work jobs that drain us for money we spend on therapy to cope with the jobs, and we try to impress people we don't even like. 

Prof Y Not dives deep into the **Imposter Syndrome** with a razor-sharp wit that cuts through the noise of traditional self-help. Instead of telling you to "manifest abundance" or "wake up at 4 AM," this book gives you permission to be human, flawed, and absolutely hilarious.
