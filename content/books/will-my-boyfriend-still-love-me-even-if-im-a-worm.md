## Are You Ready to Laugh at Your Own Misery?

The ultimate satirical relationship loyalty test. Would he still love you if you were a worm? A slug? A sentient houseplant? Time to find out.

In a world obsessed with love languages, attachment styles, and relationship podcasts, **Will My Boyfriend Still Love Me Even If I'm a Worm?** comes as a breath of fresh air (or a sigh of relief). This isn't just a book; it's a mirror reflecting the hilarity of our modern relationship anxieties.

### Why You Need This Book

Let's face it: love is absurd. We stalk our partner's Spotify playlists for emotional clues, we overthink the difference between "ok" and "okay" in texts, and we craft hypothetical scenarios to test loyalty levels that would make the CIA jealous.

Academic Satire dives deep into **Relationship Anxiety & Loyalty Tests** with a razor-sharp wit that cuts through the noise of traditional relationship advice. Instead of telling you to "trust the process" or "communicate your needs," this book gives you permission to be paranoid, overthinking, and absolutely hilarious about it.
