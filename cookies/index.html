Cookie Policy

Last Updated: February 1, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Academic Satire (“we,” “us,” or “our”) uses cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit our website academicsatire.com (the “Site”).

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and understand how you use the site.

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the Site to function properly. They enable basic features like page navigation and access to secure areas.

These cookies cannot be disabled.

Third-Party Cookies

Our Site may include content from third parties that may set their own cookies:

  • Amazon: When you click Amazon affiliate links
  • Social Media: Share buttons may set cookies
  • YouTube/Vimeo: Embedded videos may set cookies

How to Manage Cookies

Using Our Cookie Banner

When you first visit our Site, you’ll see a cookie consent banner. You can:

  • Accept All: Allow all cookies
  • Reject All: Only essential cookies will be used
  • Customize: Choose which types of cookies to allow

Browser Settings

You can also control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies
  • Firefox: Options > Privacy & Security > Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies
  • Edge: Settings > Privacy & Services > Cookies

Opt-Out Links

  • Google Analytics Opt-out
  • Your Online Choices (EU)
  • Network Advertising Initiative

Do Not Track

Our Site respects Do Not Track (DNT) signals. If your browser sends a DNT signal, we will not use analytics cookies.

Impact of Disabling Cookies

If you disable cookies, some features of our Site may not function properly:

  • Your theme preference won’t be saved
  • You may see the cookie consent banner repeatedly
  • Some interactive features may not work

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
