Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 1, 2026

Academic Satire (“we,” “us,” or “our”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website academicsatire.com (the “Site”).

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

We collect information you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Subscribe to our newsletter: Email address and name
  • Contact us: Name, email address, and message content
  • Comment on blog posts: Name, email address, and comment content

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our Site, we automatically collect certain information:

  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, device type
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on pages, click patterns
  • Location Data: General geographic location based on IP address
  • Cookies and Similar Technologies: See Section 4 below

1.3 Information from Third Parties

We may receive information from:

  • Analytics Providers: Google Analytics provides aggregated website usage data
  • Social Media Platforms: If you interact with our social media content

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Deliver newsletter content you’ve subscribed to
  • Respond to your inquiries and communications
  • Improve our website and content
  • Analyze website traffic and usage patterns
  • Prevent fraud and ensure website security
  • Comply with legal obligations

Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), we process your data based on:

  • Consent: Newsletter subscriptions and optional cookies
  • Legitimate Interest: Website analytics and improvement
  • Contractual Necessity: Fulfilling your requests
  • Legal Obligation: Compliance with applicable laws

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

3.1 Service Providers

  • Email Service: [Your email provider] for newsletter delivery
  • Analytics: Google Analytics for website statistics
  • Hosting: GitHub Pages for website hosting
  • API Services: DeepSeek for AI-generated content

3.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal process
  • Protect our rights and safety
  • Prevent fraud or illegal activity

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these sites. Please review their privacy policies.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for:

  • Newsletter subscribers: Until you unsubscribe
  • Contact form submissions: 2 years
  • Analytics data: 26 months (Google Analytics default)

7. Your Rights

7.1 For All Users

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Unsubscribe from communications
  • Request data deletion

7.2 Additional Rights for EEA Residents (GDPR)

  • Right to Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Right to Restriction: Limit how we process your data
  • Right to Object: Object to certain processing activities
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: At any time, without affecting prior processing

7.3 California Residents (CCPA)

California residents have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected
  • Know if personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Say no to the sale of personal information
  • Access their personal information
  • Equal service and price

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy [at] profynot [dot] com.

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures including:

  • HTTPS encryption
  • Secure data storage
  • Limited access to personal data
  • Regular security reviews

However, no internet transmission is 100% secure.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your residence, including the United States. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

10. Children’s Privacy

Our Site is not intended for children under 13 (or 16 in the EEA). We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

11. AI-Generated Content

Our blog uses DeepSeek API to generate content. This process:

  • Does not collect personal visitor data
  • May use general topic trends for content creation
  • Does not use your personal information for AI training

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last Modified” date. Continued use of the Site constitutes acceptance of changes.
